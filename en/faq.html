F.A.Q

Frequently asked questions

When is the best time to buy/contract sunflower seeds?
The best time to buy or contract sunflower seeds is in late August or early September. This is when the fresh harvest is available, allowing for more favorable deals.
Can I set a fixed price for an annual contract?
Yes, we can discuss the possibility of setting a fixed price for an annual contract, depending on the supply volumes and market conditions. Please contact us for more detailed information.
Can you send laboratory tests before delivery?
Yes, we can provide laboratory test results for product quality before shipping, so you can ensure that your order meets the quality standards.
Can I use your product directly for consumption?
Our sunflower seeds are intended for further processing, but if you order kernels or food-grade seeds, they can be used for direct consumption.
What are the delivery terms? (e.g., EXW or DAP?)
We offer flexible delivery terms. By agreement, we can arrange delivery under EXW (Ex Works) or DAP (Delivered At Place) terms.
